Targets and Indicators

Targets Indicators
Target 15.1 By 2020, guarantee the protection, restoration, and sustainable utilization of land and freshwater ecosystems, including forests, wetlands, mountains, and drylands, in accordance with international agreements. The percentage of the total land area that is covered by forests.
The percentage of significant sites for terrestrial and freshwater biodiversity that are designated as protected areas, categorized by ecosystem type.
Target 15.2 By 2020, encourage the adoption of sustainable forest management practices, stop deforestation, restore degraded forests, and significantly boost global efforts in afforestation and reforestation. Progress towards sustainable forest management
Target 15.3 Here's an alternative version of the sentence: By 2030, address desertification, rehabilitate degraded land and soil—including areas impacted by desertification, drought, and floods—and aim to achieve a world with no net land degradation. Percentage of the total land area that is classified as degraded.
Target 15.4 By 2030, secure the preservation of mountain ecosystems, including their biodiversity, to enhance their ability to deliver essential benefits for sustainable development The extent to which important sites for mountain biodiversity are covered by protected areas.
Mountain Green Cover Index
Target 15.5 Take immediate and substantial measures to reduce the degradation of natural habitats, halt biodiversity loss, and protect threatened species to prevent their extinction by 2020. Red List Index
Target 15.6 Encourage the fair and equitable distribution of benefits derived from the use of genetic resources and promote appropriate access to these resources, as agreed upon internationally. The number of countries that have implemented legislative, administrative, and policy frameworks to ensure the fair and equitable sharing of benefits.
Target 15.7 Take immediate action to stop poaching and trafficking of protected plant and animal species, and address both the demand and supply of illegal wildlife products. Percentage of traded wildlife that has been poached or illegally trafficked.
Target 15.8 By 2020, implement measures to prevent the introduction of invasive alien species and significantly reduce their impact on land and water ecosystems, while controlling or eradicating the most harmful species. The percentage of countries that have adopted relevant national legislation and allocated sufficient resources for the prevention or control of invasive alien species.
Target 15.9 By 2020, integrate ecosystem and biodiversity values into national and local planning, development processes, poverty reduction strategies and accounts. Progress towards national targets established in accordance with Aichi Biodiversity Target 2 of the Strategic Plan for Biodiversity 2011-2020
Target 15.a Mobilize and substantially increase financial resources from all available sources to support the conservation and sustainable use of biodiversity and ecosystems. Proportion of important sites for terrestrial and freshwater biodiversity that are covered by protected areas, by ecosystem type
Target 15.b Secure substantial resources from all sources and at all levels to fund sustainable forest management, and provide sufficient incentives to developing countries to promote practices such as conservation and reforestation Official development assistance and public expenditure on conservation and sustainable use of biodiversity and ecosystems
Target 15.c Strengthen global support for initiatives to combat poaching and trafficking of protected species, including by enhancing the capacity of local communities to pursue sustainable livelihood opportunities. The percentage of wildlife trade that involves poached or illicitly trafficked species.
